My Area of Expertise

CARSHARING


Although it is nice to own a Mercedes - i always believed that carsharing would succeed. Esspecialy in Asian Mega-Citys, an app-based service wins against the burdens that come along with owning a car. My prediction is that we will see more models that are mixed-formats, such as "owning" a car together with your circle of friends or neighbours.


I have been involved in b2b- and b2c-carsharing projects in
Hamburg & Seoul, both for the Daimler AG.

CONCEPT DESIGN


In my concepts i try to create something tangible, remixing vibes i experienced from the people that surround me. I then combine them with latest trends and technologies.
I have learned that good concepts can be explained in three sentences. They have to solve real problems and mature concepts address masses not niches while beiing feasible within the next three years.


SWARM ORGANIZATIONS


For my Master Thesis i looked at the way ants organise their hives and designed a collaboartion tool from the principles i discovered.

When you research about ants, you can find out that they do not push information – but they pull information. In human organizations we push; as in sending an emai to group of employees we are responsible for. My tool is intended to allow a newly established swarm organization to be based on this principle of pulling information.
